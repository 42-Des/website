  • Competition Information
    • Date: 21 Dec 2016
    • Time: 1:25pm - 4:00pm
    • Dress code:
      • Winter P.E. uniform for all competitors; winter P.E. uniform or winter  school uniform for all student officials.
      • Pupils who are having P.E. lessons that morning or the day before can wear white T-shirts and dark blue or black shorts in the P.E. lessons.
    • Weather condition: The competition will be postponed, if
      • Temperature is below 10°C with rain, or
      • Heavy rain showers
  •  Rules and regulations
    • Grouping
      • House competition
        • Junior Boys S1
        • Junior Girls S1
        • Junior Boys S2
        • Junior Girls S2
        • Senior Boys S3-4
        • Senior Girls S3-4
        • Senior Boys S5-6
        • Senior Girls S5-6
      • Class competition
        • Junior- S1
        • Junior- S2
        • Senior- S3-S4
        • Senior- S5-S6                
    • Point System
      • House competition

The first 12 of each team have to count for points. The House with the least points will be the champion. In case of a tie between two teams, it shall be resolved in favour of the team whose last scoring member among the first 12 competitors finished nearer the first place. Team with less than 12 runners will be disqualified.

      • Class competition

The first 3 boys and first 3 girls of a class have to count for points.
The class with the least points will be the champion. In case of a tie between two teams, it shall be resolved in favour of the team whose last scoring member among the first competitors finished nearer the first place. Team with less than 6 runners (3 boys and 3 girls) will be disqualified.

    • Awards
      • House competition
  • Certificates - first ten places in each group
  • Medals - First, Second and Third places in each group
  • Overall Champion award - House with the highest total scores
  • The Most Participation Award - House with the highest percentage of competitors
      • Class competition
  • Prizes - First, Second and Third places in each group
  • The Most Participation Award - class in the two Senior groups with the highest percentage of participation
